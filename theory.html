Magnetic Field Theory

Magnetic Field Intensity

Magnetic field strength measured by the sensor is represented as:

\\[ |B| = \\sqrt{B_x^2 + B_y^2 + B_z^2} \\]

Where \\(B_x, B_y, B_z\\) are the components measured along each axis.

Ampere’s Law

\\[ B = \\frac{\\mu_0 I}{2\\pi r} \\]

This explains how electric current generates magnetic fields.
